Pizza City

  • 4 Players
  • 30 Min
  • Tile Placement & Hand Management

Become the pizza (delivery) champion in an ever-changing city.

Pizza City is a game for 4 players in two teams of two. On each team players are either a Pizza Courier, trying to navigate the streets of Pizza City on bikes to deliver pizzas, or a City Planner who’s constantly changing the landscape of the city to help their teammate and hinder their opponent.

In the Box

56 card Bike Deck
60 card City Deck
Board with roads and city blocks
2 Bike Tokens
7 Pizza Drop-Off Tokens
4 Reference cards
