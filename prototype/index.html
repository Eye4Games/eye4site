About Us

We’re a company, born of collaboration, and forged in the ashes of a dying star. The dying star is a metaphor for college. Since emerging from that star we’ve come to meet new people and collaborate with each other all across the New England area and beyond. Most of us are in Boston these days though.

We strive to create games centered around a unique vision and theme. The art, flavor, and mechanics designed to complement each other for engaging gameplay experiences.
